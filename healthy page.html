Morning Routine

  1. Wake up early – Aim for 6–7 AM
  2. Hydrate – Drink a glass of water
  3. Stretch / Exercise – 10–20 minutes
  4. Healthy Breakfast – Protein & fruits
  5. Plan your day – Top 3 priorities

Daytime Routine

  1. Focus sessions – Use time blocks
  2. Healthy snacks – Fruits, nuts
  3. Hydrate regularly
  4. Short breaks every hour
  5. Get sunlight / fresh air

Afternoon Routine

  1. Balanced lunch
  2. Power nap (10–20 min)
  3. Check progress

Evening Routine

  1. Exercise / Walk
  2. Light healthy dinner
  3. Reflect on the day
  4. Digital detox

Night Routine

  1. Prepare for next day
  2. Relaxation (reading/meditation)
  3. Sleep 7–8 hours
